/// ROBOWAR

The Robo-Battle is an exciting robotics competition where teams design and build combat robots that engage in thrilling battles within an arena. This event tests engineering skills, strategy, and teamwork. The event is spans across two days with the first day featuring a Robo-race and day two brings the epic showdown battle. The Robo-Battle emphasizes practical application of engineering skills, teamwork, and strategic thinking, aligning with our college's commitment to fostering technical excellence.

Problem Statements

  • Teams consist of a maximum of four individuals. Each team must have a minimum of two members.
  • Robots are built to engage in combat, and the last robot standing or the one that incapacitates its opponents wins the battle.
  • There are specific rules for robot design and combat safety to ensure a fair and safe competition.

/// BID BOUNDARIES

The "Auction" takes inspiration from the high-energy world of IPL auctions but with a unique twist—participants bid for virtual players. This exciting game adds a layer of strategy and excitement as participants strategically bid for players, aiming to create the most formidable team. The "Auction" not only adds an exciting dimension to financial literacy but also emphasizes strategic decision-making in a dynamic context. This innovative activity aligns with our commitment to providing diverse and engaging learning experiences.

RULES

  • Open to all attendees in individual participation, "Auction" transforms the concept of player bidding into an engaging and accessible activity, inviting participants to experience the thrill of strategic decision-making.
  • Participants are given a set of virtual players to bid on, and they compete through strategic bidding using virtual currency. The twist lies in the IPL format, where the participant with the highest total stat of all purchased players emerges victorious.
