IEEE BKBIET STUDENT BRANCH

IEEE BKBIET is a technical student community that functions as a team at B K Birla Institute of Engineering and Technology located in Pilani, Rajasthan (India). The chapter is in action since the year 2008 and has achieved great hieghts. The Execomm team Focuses on overall development that enhance the technological and innovation instincts of the students has been successful in doing so since many years. Every year IEEE BKBIET hosts IEEE THE VIBES, the annual technical fest which is three to four full days festivities ranging from workshops, seminars, computer coding languages, treasure hunts, technical extravaganzas like sessions with the scientists and high class dignitaries on cutting edge issues and topics related to our industry. There are also many opportunities for networking and socialising with our colleagues.

GDSC BKBIET

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at B. K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology (BKBIET) stands as a beacon of technological excellence within the college community. Led by dynamic individuals with a shared passion for innovation, GDSC BKBIET has curated a rich history of organizing diverse events that cater to a wide spectrum of technical interests. From hackathons and workshops to engaging speaker sessions, the club is dedicated to fostering an environment where students can collaborate, share ideas, and continuously explore the latest trends in the tech industry.

B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology

B K Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology, Pilani, established in 2007 by Shree Krishnapan Charity Trust under the Chairmanship of Late Syt. Basant Kumar Birla, is a renowned institution of international repute. Approved by AICTE and affiliated with Bikaner Technical University, it offers master's degree programs in Computer Science Engineering and VLSI Design and Technology, along with undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. The institute boasts dedicated faculties, modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and libraries. Accredited by NBA, it provides quality education in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, VLSI Design, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and specialized areas like Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. With sprawling facilities over 18 acres, including hostels, gymnasiums, playgrounds, and proximity to other prestigious campuses.
