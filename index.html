ANANTAM is a collaborative and grand-scale technical fest organized by IEEE BKBIET Student Branch and GDSC BKBIET Techfest. It features a diverse range of events, including a continuous four-day hackathon, robo race, robo war, DSA competition, Teach Quiz, various speaker sessions covering technical, non-technical, and administrative themes, Dalal Street (related to finance and stock market), Auction (related to IPL Auction), UX/UI design challenge, Murder Mystery (related to solving crime cases). Additionally, the fest includes fun activities like footloose, open mic, bonfire, anime quiz, and panel discussions with eminent guests and speakers. ANANTAM is a fusion of the successful annual tech fests, VIBES by IEEE BKBIET SB and Zenith by GDSC BKBIET, creating a comprehensive and engaging experience for participants.
